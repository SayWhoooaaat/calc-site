Chamber Furnaces

2 Chamber furnaces with automatic loading unit in front. Place products on racks on loading stations, and the automatic handler will insert it into the furnace efficiently and precisely. All product batches will have the exact same retention time and quality.

These furnaces are heat treating aluminum car parts for a premium european car brand.
